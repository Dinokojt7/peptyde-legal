2. Information We Collect›

Account Information We may collect your name, email address, authentication provider (Apple or Google), account ID, profile photo URL, and basic account settings. We use this to create and manage your account, authenticate you, sync your data, and provide support. Onboarding Information We may collect information you provide during onboarding, such as goals, preferences, peptide experience, and settings, to personalise your App experience. User-Entered Tracking Data Peptyde may collect health-related tracking data you choose to enter, including: • Peptide names and inventory information • Dose logs and dose history • Injection sites • Side effects • Hydration entries • Notes and reminders This information is used to provide Peptyde's core tracking, organisation, reminder, history, and personalisation features. Pep Bot & AI Messages If you use Pep Bot or other AI features, your messages and limited session context may be sent to an AI provider to generate a response. We do not currently use Pep Bot messages or personal health data to train Peptyde-owned AI models. Subscription Information Subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and RevenueCat. We may receive subscription status, entitlement status, product identifier, renewal status, and purchase metadata. We do not receive your full payment card information. Device & Technical Information We may collect device type, operating system, app version, crash reports, performance logs, and basic diagnostics to operate and improve the App. Support Communications If you contact Peptyde for support, we may collect the information you choose to include in your message. Please avoid sending unnecessary health information through support requests.

We use health data to: • Provide Peptyde's core tracking features. • Help you organise logs, history, inventory, and reminders. • Sync your data across devices. • Provide AI features you request. • Personalise your App experience. • Improve App functionality and reliability. • Maintain security and prevent misuse. • Respond to support requests. • Comply with legal obligations. 6. Data Storage & Security›

Your account and tracking data are stored on Firebase / Google Cloud servers. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit and encryption at rest where supported by our service providers. No system can guarantee absolute security. If a data breach requires notification under applicable law, we will notify affected users as required. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat, Apple, and Google. We do not receive or store your full payment card information. Analytics and crash reporting are processed by Firebase. We do not intentionally send health entries, dose logs, or sensitive tracking details to third-party analytics providers.

Upon account deletion request: • Active system data is deleted or deactivated within a reasonable period (generally within 30 days). • Backup copies may persist for a limited period before being overwritten. • Some information may be retained for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, or compliance purposes. • Data already processed by third-party service providers may remain subject to their own retention schedules.
